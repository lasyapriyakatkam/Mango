# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
Mango project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being kind, welcoming, and patient — especially with newcomers.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Instances of abuse may be reported to the project maintainer via a GitHub issue
or direct contact. Maintainers will review and respond to reports and may take
any action they deem appropriate, including warning or banning offenders.
